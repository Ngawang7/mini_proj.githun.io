Rental FAQs

What's included in the rent?

This varies by property. Most include water and trash service. Some may include additional utilities. Our listings clearly specify what's included for each property.

How long are lease terms?

Standard leases are 12 months, but we also offer flexible terms including 6-month and month-to-month options (premium may apply).

What are the application requirements?

We require proof of income (3x monthly rent), good rental history, and a credit check. Co-signers are accepted for those who don't meet requirements.

How do I schedule a viewing?

Click the "Schedule Viewing" button on any listing or call our rental hotline at (123) 456-7890. We offer in-person and virtual tours.
